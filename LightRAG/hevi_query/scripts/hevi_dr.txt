=== TURNER (2003) DEFINITION ===

Dose-Response is the causal translation function from hazard to impact through the coupled human-technical system. It answers: how does the SCALE, INTENSITY, or presence of the hazard translate into the magnitude of the impact, given the specific exposure scenario and vulnerability conditions? It is NOT a restatement of vulnerability or impact. It is the causal arc that LINKS them.

In the original RH model, dose-response = f(exposure × sensitivity). It captures the transformation: input (hazard + exposure context) → mechanism (vulnerability triggered) → output (impact magnitude).
